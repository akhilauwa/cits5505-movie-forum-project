Scoop

"How the BBC obtained the bombshell interview with Prince Andrew about his friendship with convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein."

Director: Philip Martin


Writers: Samantha McAlisterPeter MoffatGeoff Bussetil


Stars: Billie PiperGillian AndersonRufus Sewell



Movie Introduction

In 2010, NYC photographer Jae Donnelly photographs Prince Andrew with Jeffrey Epstein, stirring controversy. Nine years later, BBC producer Sam McAlister, while facing staff cuts, spots this photo and seeks to secure an interview. Despite palace attempts to refurbish Andrew’s image, revelations from Donnelly about Epstein's activities add urgency. Sam navigates complex negotiations with Andrew's team and, despite internal tensions at BBC, secures a groundbreaking interview. Aired in 2019, the interview exposes Andrew's dubious defenses about his ties with Epstein and leads to his public downfall. Andrew steps down from royal duties following the disastrous PR and later settles a sexual assault lawsuit for £12 million in 2022. Sam leaves the BBC to teach negotiation.
