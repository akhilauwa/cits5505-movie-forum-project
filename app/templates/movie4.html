Kung Fu Panda 4

"After Po is tapped to become the Spiritual Leader of the Valley of Peace, he needs to find and train a new Dragon Warrior, while a wicked sorceress plans to re-summon all the master villains whom Po has vanquished to the spirit realm."

Directors: Mike MitchellStephanie Stine

Movie Introduction

In the Valley of Peace, Po is tasked by Master Shifu to find a successor as he transitions to a spiritual leader. During this time, Po encounters Zhen, a fox bandit, and apprehends her after she attempts to steal ancient weapons. However, they learn that Tai Lung's supposed return was orchestrated by The Chameleon, a shape-shifting sorceress. Po and Zhen travel to Juniper City to confront her, where Po is deceived and nearly killed. With Zhen’s change of heart and the aid of Po's fathers, they challenge The Chameleon. Zhen eventually helps Po defeat The Chameleon, who had summoned past kung fu masters using Po’s staff. After restoring peace and returning the stolen powers, Po names Zhen as his successor, and with the Furious Five, begins training her to become the new Dragon Warrior.
