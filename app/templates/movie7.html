Drift

"Jacqueline escapes her war-torn country to a Greek island. She meets an unmoored tour guide and the two become close as they each find hope in the other."

Director: Anthony Chen


Writers: Susanne FarrellAlexander Maksik


Stars: Adonis SklavenasAdriana TeplytskaAggelos Georgiadis



Movie Introduction

The film follows Jacqueline, a Liberian refugee, who flees to a Greek island and develops a friendship with an American tour guide while coping with her past.
