The Greatest Hits

"A love story centering on the connection between music and memory and how they transport us, sometimes literally."

Director: Ned Benson


Writer: Ned Benson


Stars: Lucy BoyntonJustin H. MinDavid Corenswet



Movie Introduction

Harriet, still grieving her boyfriend Max who died in a car crash, has the ability to time travel when she hears songs associated with him. To avoid unwanted time travel, she wears noise-canceling headphones. Despite attempts to save Max, Harriet meets David at a grief support group and they gradually grow close. After revealing her time-travel ability to David, she decides not to change the past to ensure her present with David could exist. In this new reality, Harriet no longer needs headphones and sees Max with someone else, untroubled. She then reconnects with David at a concert, hinting at a new beginning.
