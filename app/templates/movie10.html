Shirley

"A famous horror writer finds inspiration for her next book after she and her husband take in a young couple."

Director: Josephine Decker


Writers: Sarah GubbinsSusan Scarf Merrell


Stars: Elisabeth MossOdessa YoungMichael Stuhlbarg



Movie Introduction

Fred and Rose Nemser move to Bennington College for Fred's job, living with the famous writer Shirley Jackson and her husband Stanley Hyman. Shirley, suffering from agoraphobia and writer's block, coldly manipulates Rose into becoming the household caretaker. As Shirley resumes writing, inspired by the disappearance of a student named Paula Jean Welden, Rose becomes her reluctant muse and assistant. The intense relationship culminates in Rose confronting personal and professional betrayals by both Stanley and her own husband, Fred. Ultimately, Rose rejects her subservient domestic role and leaves with Fred, determined to embrace a new life of independence. Shirley continues her complicated marriage with Stanley, celebrating the completion of her novel, "Hangsaman," which Stanley praises as a masterpiece.
