Rebel Moon

"When a peaceful settlement on the edge of a distant moon finds itself threatened by a tyrannical ruling force, a stranger living among its villagers becomes their best hope for survival."

Director: Zack Snyder


Writers: Zack SnyderKurt JohnstadShay Hatten


Stars: Sofia BoutellaDjimon HounsouEd Skrein



Movie Introduction

Atticus Noble, an admiral from the Imperium, arrives on the moon Veldt to suppress a rebel group and requisitions grain from the villagers, leading to the village leader Sindri's death. Kora, a former Imperium soldier turned villager, retaliates by killing Imperial soldiers with help from a defected robot named Jimmy. To defend against Noble’s imminent return, Kora and another villager Gunnar recruit fighters including a disgraced general, Titus, and other skilled warriors. Despite internal betrayals and a devastating attack by Noble, Kora and her allies manage to kill Noble in a fierce battle. However, Noble is later resurrected by the Imperium's forces, continuing the cycle of conflict.
