Ghostbusters: Frozen Empire

"When the discovery of an ancient artifact unleashes an evil force, Ghostbusters new and old must join forces to protect their home and save the world from a second ice age."

Director: Gil Kenan


Writers: Gil KenanJason ReitmanIvan Reitman


Stars: Paul RuddCarrie CoonFinn Wolfhard



Movie Introduction

Three years after Summerville, Callie Spengler, her family, and friends move to New York City to help reestablish the Ghostbusters. After capturing a ghost, they face shutdown threats from Mayor Walter Peck. Meanwhile, Ray and Podcast acquire a strange orb that unleashes a psionic pulse, threatening their ghost containment unit. The orb, built to imprison the demonic god Garraka, is revealed to have been safeguarded by Nadeem’s grandmother. Garraka escapes and freezes the city, seeking to unleash more ghosts. Phoebe, using a proton pack modified with copper alloys, and Nadeem, wielding his grandmother’s brass armor, manage to weaken Garraka. With help from the betrayed ghost Melody, the Ghostbusters trap Garraka, saving the city and securing their reinstatement. As New York thaws, they are hailed as heroes, continuing their adventures.
