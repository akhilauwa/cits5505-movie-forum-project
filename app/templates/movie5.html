Which Brings Me To You

"Two romantic burnouts meet at a wedding and almost hook up in the coatroom before putting the brakes on. They agree to exchange candid confessions about their pasts on the off chance that this might be the real thing."

Director: Peter Hutchings


Writers: Steve AlmondJulianna BaggottKeith Bunin


Stars: Lucy HaleNat WolffJohn Gallagher Jr.



Movie Introduction

At a wedding in The Hamptons, photographer Will meets journalist Jane. After an interrupted closet tryst, they share intimate stories of past relationships and heartbreaks at a diner. Will reveals a previous engagement and a daughter he hasn't mentioned, causing Jane to reconsider their connection. However, after discovering the depth of Will's love for his daughter and sharing more personal revelations, they find themselves drawn to each other. Acknowledging their past scars, they decide to take a chance on a future together, culminating in a kiss and a promise to leap into this new relationship.
